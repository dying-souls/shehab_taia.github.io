About Me
Engineer by training,
builder by nature.

I'm a Mechatronics MEng student at the University of Birmingham — a discipline that sits at the intersection of mechanical engineering, electronics, and software. I care deeply about understanding the why behind systems, not just making them work.

I've worked in IT support at Talaat Moustafa Group and I'm actively building hands-on experience across embedded systems, motor control, sensors, and prototyping. I enjoy the challenge of making things that bridge the physical and digital world.

When I'm not in the lab, I'm watching video essays, thinking about F1 aero, or reading about biology. I believe the best engineers are curious about everything.
